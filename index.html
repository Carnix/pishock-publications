Demons & Angels

By Dennis Pishock

Dennis Pishock served for 18 years as a fightfighter and is active law enforcement today. After thousands of traumatic calls, after seeing friends and their families fall apart, he is sharing his stories and his methods for coping while remaining on the front lines. Delve into Pishock's most personal demons and maybe find some of the light he has found.

This is my personal journey and a book intended to help not only myself but every LEO and firefighter who has worn the badge, spending countless sleepless nights away from their families. I believe this book will also resonate with our military personnel and anyone who has chosen to serve this great country. I have trained with and alongside every branch of our exceptional military. Every military member I've spoken with has told me the same thing: they chose public safety to regain the brotherhood they had lost. Servitude is where "it's at," and you either understand that or you don't. To those who serve, I love you and look forward to meeting you all in person someday. To those who purposely choose not to embrace the honor of serving, you've truly missed out — you'll never understand.
